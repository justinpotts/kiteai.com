Pricing

Free, for a weekend project (5,000 calls / month)

For your apps that aren't quite ready for launch, or just a side project you're interested in, free apps are for you. Limited to 5,000 calls per month, we provide just enought to make it functional and keep the cost low enough you can enjoy it for free.

Pro, for your side hustle ($1.20 / 1,000 calls)

You're a hustler, burning the midnight oil, working on your world-changing app. Create as many keys as you'd like, and enjoy the full usage of the API.

Enterprise, for the big companies

Representing a medium to large corporation? Working on an app and expecting to use more than 1,000,000 calls per month? Get in touch and let's set up a meeting to talk about how we can work together. We'll provide priority support and work closely with your team to ensure a smooth integration.
